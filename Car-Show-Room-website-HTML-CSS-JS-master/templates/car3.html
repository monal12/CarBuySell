Land Range Rover

Price: ₹2000k Latest Update: The variant-wise prices of the 2022 Range Rover are out and its deliveries are now underway across India.
Land Rover Range Rover Price: The Range Rover is listed from Rs 2.32 crore (ex-showroom) onwards.
Land Rover Range Rover Variants: The fifth-gen Range Rover is now available in five trims: SE, HSE, Autobiography, First Edition and SV.
Land Rover Range Rover Seating Capacity: Land Rover is offering the SUV in multiple seating configurations: 4-seater, 5-seater, and 7-seater.
Land Rover Range Rover Engine and Transmission: In terms of powertrains, it is available with a mix of petrol and diesel engines, both with 48V mild-hybrid tech. All engines are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The 3-litre six-cylinder petrol makes 400PS/550Nm and the 3-litre diesel produces 351PS/700Nm. Its flagship variant is equipped with the 4.4-litre twin-turbo V8 producing 530PS/750Nm.
Land Rover Range Rover Features: Range Rover gets a 13.7-inch digital driver’s display, a 13.1-inch touchscreen infotainment system, a 1600W Meridian sound system, and Amazon-Alexa connectivity.
Land Rover Range Rover Rivals: It rivals the Lexus LX and Mercedes-Benz Maybach GLS. The sporty variant with the twin-turbo V8 also takes on the Aston Martin DBX and Bentley Bentayga.
